1. Enter how long you would like your workout to be in minutes.
    We recommend something between 5-20 minutes.
  2. Your workout will be randomly generated based on the time specified.
  3. Select the number of circuits you would like to complete.
    i.e. if your workout is 5 minutes and you select 2 circuits,
    you will perform those 5 exercises twice for a total of 10 minutes.
  4. Each exercise will be performed for 45 seconds, followed by a 15 second rest.
  5. Once complete, you may save your workout by clicking the save button. Enjoy!
